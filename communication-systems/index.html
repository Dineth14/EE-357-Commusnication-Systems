Key Equations at a Glance

AM Signal $$s(t) = A_c[1+\mu\cos(2\pi f_m t)]\cos(2\pi f_c t)$$ Carrier amplitude modulated by message
FM Signal $$s(t) = A_c\cos\!\left[2\pi f_c t + 2\pi k_f \int_0^t m(\tau)\,d\tau\right]$$ Frequency deviation tracks the message
PCM SQNR $$\text{SQNR} \approx 1.76 + 6.02n \text{ dB}$$ Quantization quality scales with bit depth
Nyquist Criterion $$\sum_{k} P\!\left(f - \frac{k}{T_b}\right) = T_b$$ ISI-free pulse shaping condition
Shannon Capacity $$C = B\log_2(1 + \text{SNR})$$ Channel capacity: the ultimate limit
BER (BPSK) $$P_b = Q\!\left(\sqrt{\frac{2E_b}{N_0}}\right)$$ BPSK has the best BER for binary modulation
